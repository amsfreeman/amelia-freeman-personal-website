Post College Years

In May of 2015, I graduated from the University of Arkansas with my history degree. I worked for a period of time at a company in Kansas City, and then was an Au-Pair in Munich, Germany for the whole year of 2017.

After returning from Munich, I worked as a Recruiter at UnitedLex for three years. Following this period, I moved back to Germany and lived there from May 2021-December 2022.

I returned to the US in December 2022, and since then have been working to learn software development by coding using CodeCademy.com. During this time period, I also spoke to a variety of people regarding how to bet get into a career in software development. Ultimately, I chose to enroll in Flatiron's Software Engineering Bootcamp. I am excited for this new adventure.
